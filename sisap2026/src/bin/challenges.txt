🔹 Task 1: K-Nearest Neighbor Graph (Metric Self-Join)
Revisiting a classic problem in high dimensions. Develop a memory-efficient index to approximate the k-NN graph (k=15) on 6.4M Wikipedia vectors (1024 dims).

🔹 Task 2: Maximum Inner Product Search on LLM attention workloads
Search under distribution shift! Solve Maximum Inner Product Search for queries where the distribution differs from the index, using a dataset retrieved from Llama-3-8B-262k (262k vectors, 128 dims) dataset.

🔹 Task 3: Indexing Very Sparse High-Dimensional Vectors
Overcoming the challenge of sparse representations. Tackle the high dimensionality and learned term distributions of SPLADE-v3 sparse embeddings on the Natural Questions (NQ) dataset (2.68M vectors, ~30k dims).
